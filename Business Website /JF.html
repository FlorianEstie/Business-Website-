TEAM

We are James and Florian, founders of The Coffee Code. We are an Amsterdam-based Start-up company in the coffee industry. With our product we want to reach a large audience and make them aware of the biological way in which we produce coffee. The Coffee Code hopes to dominate the coffee market in an organic way within a period of ten years. Until then, we will do our best every day to achieve this goal!
