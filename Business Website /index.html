OUR STORY

Our journey began nearly two weeks ago in Amsterdam when our founders James and Florian – then traders of organic sesame seeds – stumbled upon a hillside of beautiful coffee trees ripe with red cherries. It was love at first taste.

WHO ARE WE?

We are the first speciality coffee roaster in the Netherlands to manage the entire bean-to-cup process. Our roasters roast to bring out the best and full potential of what’s inherent in each particular coffee already. They know exactly how to do this because they have been doing this for years.
